Saxophonist. Educator. Composer.

Devin is a highly accomplished saxophonist based in the UK, hailing originally from the USA. With an impressive 20 years of experience under his belt, With an impressive 20 years of experience under his belt, he's not just a saxophonist; he's a musical virtuoso with the power to elevate any event.

Devin's journey in music began with a profound love for travel and adventure, leading him to earning a B.M. in Jazz Studies from the esteemed University of North Texas and then touring the world as a cruise ship performer. Throughout his career he has played a wide variety of music; from pit orchestras, to local clubs and festivals, to private weddings and corporate events. Currently, he's the resident saxophonist at Kioki Nightclub, where his musical prowess continues to captivate audiences. Devin's journey is a testament to his unwavering dedication to the art of music, and he's ready to infuse your event with unforgettable melodies and an unparalleled musical experience.
